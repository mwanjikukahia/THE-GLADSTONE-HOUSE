MEETINGS & EVENTS

Looking for the perfect venue for your meeting or event? The Gladstone House and Resort is home to the most spacious and lucrative corporate meetings & events management venues.

We have designed rooms ideal for board meetings, small and medium size business meetings, all the meeting rooms feature state-of-the-art technology to meet the expectations of the business travellers as well as to provide excellent service and comfortable settings.

All meeting rooms are equipped with general meeting equipment such as a screen, overhead projectors, whiteboard, slide projector, barcovision, sound system, wireless microphone, DVD and VCR player, airconditioning and a lighting system. With a large inventory of equipment, we help our guests achieve their meeting goals with creativity and professionalism.

We also have grounds ideal for outdoor events such as weddings and team building activities.

Whether you are hosting 20, 200, or 2000, launching a new product, hosting a national sales meeting, creating a special customer event, recognizing achievement, holding a wedding, a birthday ceebration, or arranging medical discovery meetings, The Gladstone House and Resort is the right place to create an incredible experience that meets your needs.
