WELCOME TO THE GLADSTONE HOUSE RESORT.

Discretely tucked at the edge of a mini-forest right next to the Nairobi-Nanyuki highway, Marua-Kiganjo road lies a unique hotel where nature and luxury merge for an exceptional sensory experience. Discover this urban family holiday sanctuary; the ultimate hub of energy and style.

This is an ideal base for business travellers, family events and holiday seekers. Whether your stay is long or short, there’s a feeling of ease about this environment, with a vibe that's truly one of a kind and prestige that accompanies period décor.

Featuring a pleasant blend of functionality, modern feel and elegance, the decor and furnishings create an inviting ambience fostering relaxation and a great sense of well-being from the moment you check in. With a the diverse blend of rooms options, you will definitely find that ideal accommodation option that suites your taste and budget.
