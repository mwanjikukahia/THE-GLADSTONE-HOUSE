About Us.

Tranquillity and Happinness are the first words that spring to mind when you think of Gladstone House and RESORT. Set in Marua - Nanyuki road 750 metres from the main road, the experience here is rated as exceptional. We offer high quality services from our in-house teams, as well as the utmost comfort and discretion.


Your wellbeing and sustainability are also at the core of our DNA. Our rooms and suites have state-of-the-art wellness amenities and are artistically curated to reflect its natural surroundings.
